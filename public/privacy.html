Privacy Policy

NextOffer.ai — last updated June 2026

This policy describes how NextOffer.ai (“we”, “us”) collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use our job search and AI application-kit service at nextoffer.ai (the “Service”).

1. Who we are

The Service is operated by NextOffer.ai. For privacy requests, contact ranurainfotech@gmail.com.

2. Information we collect

  • Account data — When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and Google account identifier (via Firebase Authentication).
  • Resume and profile — Text you paste or upload (PDF) is stored on our servers to parse your skills, experience, and job preferences and to generate tailored documents.
  • Job search activity — Filters you select, search usage counts, and metadata about saved application kits (job title, company, document types generated).
  • Payment status — If you subscribe to Pro, we store subscription tier and status from our payment provider. We do not store full card numbers.
  • Technical and usage data — If you accept analytics cookies, we may collect app events (e.g. screen views, feature usage) via Google Analytics 4. We do not send resume text or full job descriptions in analytics events.
  • Support communications — Information you send to ranurainfotech@gmail.com.

3. How we use your information

  • Provide job search results and AI-generated resumes, cover letters, and emails tailored to your profile.
  • Enforce free-tier limits and manage Pro subscriptions.
  • Improve reliability, security, and product experience (including aggregated analytics, if consented).
  • Respond to support and legal requests.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your resume for third-party advertising profiles.

4. AI processing

Resume parsing, job matching scores, and document generation use artificial intelligence (including models provided by Anthropic). Your resume text and relevant job details are sent to our servers and to AI providers only to deliver the Service. Outputs are generated automatically and may contain errors — see our Terms of Service.

5. Third-party service providers

We rely on trusted processors to run the Service. They process data on our instructions and under their own terms and privacy policies:

  • Google / Firebase — authentication, hosting, database (Firestore), and optional analytics (GA4).
  • Anthropic — AI text generation and resume parsing.
  • RapidAPI / JSearch (OpenWeb Ninja) — job listing search; queries may include job title and location filters (not your full resume).
  • Razorpay — subscription checkout and billing (UPI, cards, netbanking).

Job listings and apply links originate from third-party job boards; their sites have separate privacy practices when you leave our Service.

6. Data retention

  • Account and profile — Kept while your account is active. You may delete resume data or your entire account in the app (Settings → Privacy & data) or by emailing ranurainfotech@gmail.com.
  • Application kits — Stored until you delete your account or resume data (resume deletion removes parsed profile and uploaded resumes; kits may remain until account deletion — use “Delete account” to remove kits).
  • Usage counters — Monthly search/upload limits reset automatically; underlying logs may be retained in server logs for a limited period.
  • Analytics — Retained according to Google Analytics settings (typically up to 14 months for event data, configurable in our GA property).
  • Backups and logs — Encrypted backups and operational logs may persist for a short period after deletion before being overwritten.

7. International transfers

Our infrastructure and providers may process data in the United States and other countries. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses offered by providers) for cross-border transfers.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location (including the EEA, UK, and certain US states), you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent (e.g. analytics cookies) without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.
  • Data portability, where applicable.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

To exercise these rights, use in-app deletion tools or contact ranurainfotech@gmail.com. We will respond within a reasonable time as required by law.

9. Cookies and analytics

Essential cookies/local storage are used for sign-in session state. If our app offers analytics and you are in a region where consent is required, we ask before enabling Google Analytics. You can change your choice by clearing site data or contacting support.

10. Security

We use industry-standard measures (encryption in transit, access controls, Firebase security rules). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

11. Children

The Service is not directed to anyone under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

12. Changes

We may update this policy. The “last updated” date will change; material changes may be notified in the app or by email where appropriate.

13. Contact

Privacy questions or requests: ranurainfotech@gmail.com
